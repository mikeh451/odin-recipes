Perfectly Cooked Steak

Making the perfect steak requires practice, patience, preperation, and the right tools.

It's taken years for me to get where I'm at, but if you follow these steps you'll be there in no time.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Steak of your choice (ribeye is my favorite)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 table poons high heat oil (canola or peanut)
  • cast iron pan

Steps

  1. Take steak out of fridge, allow to rest at room temp for 15 minutes
  2. Heat oil on pan over medium high heat (you'll know it's hot enough when oil is smoking but not burning
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper on steak, rub in well on both sides
  4. Lay steak down in center of pan to start cooking, give quick stir to cover bottom with oil
  5. Cook 3-5 minutes per side for rare, longer for more well done.
  6. Flip. Cook other side same amount of time
  7. When finished, place steak on plate. Spread butter on top of steak, allow to rest 5-10 minutes.
